Attendance Rules

General Attendance 
Work Hours Compliance
Employees must complete at least 8 work hours per day (excluding lunch break).

If total work hours < (is less than) 8 hours, Considered UNDERTIME.

Flexitime starts not earlier than 7:00 AM and ends not later than 6:00 PM. 

(Example:  If an employee times in before 7:00 AM, work hours will start counting from 7:00 AM until time out)

Every Monday All Employees must time in earlier than 8 :00 AM for the flag ceremony regardless of Schedule. 

Tardiness
Employees have at least a 15 minute grace period

If time in >(exceeds past) 15 minutes allowed time range, Considered LATE (Ex: Fixed Flexi 7:00-4:00, time in at 7:15 time in : not LATE ; else if 7:16 : Late for 16 minutes.

 Duplicated Punches/ Double Punches
In case of Duplicated Time In’s, The First would always be considered as the the official time in

In case of Duplicated Time Out’s, The latter always would be considered as the official time out


Work Arrangement Type
Full Flexitime

Time in Range:
Employees may Time in Between 7:00-9:00 AM

Time out Range:
Work must end between 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM, (completing 8 hours).

Validation Rules:
If time in >(exceeds past) 9:15, Considered LATE
If time in is VALID but the total work hours <(is less than) 8 hours, Considered UNDERTIME
On Flag Ceremony days (Every Monday), flexitime is limited to 7:00AM - 8:00AM only, If time in >(exceeds past) 8:00 AM on Monday, Considered LATE.

      C. Work From Home
WFH employees must follow a fixed schedule of 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM.
Employees must log their time-in and time-out daily via the agreed 	 communication method.

Time logs must be recorded in the system and reflected in the Individual Daily Log and Accomplishment Report (IDLAR) and Daily Time Record (DTR).

If no time log or accomplishment report is submitted, the day will be marked as Absent.
